## Mom's Blueberry Muffins
#### everyone's favourite muffin!

### Ingredients
- 2 eggs
- 1 1/4 cup milk
- 1/2 cup melted butter or margarine
- 1 tsp grated lemon rind
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 1/2 cup flour
- 1 cup whole wheat flour
- 1 tbsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 1/2 cup fresh or frozen blueberries

- Fold in blueberries last, Bake at 400F for 20 min
- Makes a dozen monster muffins!
